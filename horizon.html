How do I submit a creative brief?

Simply navigate to the "Brief" section in the navigation bar or use our internal project management tool. Select your tier based on the complexity of your request, fill out the required fields, and hit submit.

What is the typical turnaround time?

Turnaround times vary by tier. Tier 4 (Self-Service) is instant. Tier 3 requests typically take 3-5 business days. Tier 1 and 2 projects are scoped individually.

External vendor selection?

Absolutely. If our internal team is at capacity or a project requires niche specialization, we manage a roster of vetted agency partners and freelancers.
